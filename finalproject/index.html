The Dune Universe has been a source of fascination since it was first outlined in 1959.

The original series was written by Frank Herbert and was composed of the following titles:

  • Dune
  • Dune Messiah
  • Children of Dune
  • God Emperor of Dune
  • Heretics of Dune
  • Chapterhouse: Dune
The last book, Chapterhouse Dune, was not intended to be the last, but Frank Herberts death in 1986 left the series unresolved.

Twenty years later his son Brian Herbert used his fathers notes to publish two sequels and finish off the series.

  • Hunters of Dune
  • Sandworms of Dune
Brian also expanded the universe of Dune through multiple prequel series, bringing more depth to the setting.
